Welcome to Timog: Quezon City's Foodie Hub

Timog Avenue, also known as Scout Area, is a food lover's paradise in Quezon City. Known for its concentrations of Korean restaurants, Filipino eateries, and lively nightlife spots, Timog offers diverse dining options for every budget and craving. Whether you're craving authentic Korean BBQ, traditional Filipino comfort food, or international flavors, Timog has it all.
