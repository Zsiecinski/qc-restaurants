Frequently Asked Questions

What types of restaurants are in Trinoma?

Trinoma offers a diverse mix of Filipino, American, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Thai, and other international cuisines, from casual food court options to sit-down restaurants.

Is parking available at Trinoma?

Yes—Trinoma Mall has several parking areas including covered parking and basement parking available for shoppers.

Are Trinoma restaurants open late?

Operating hours vary by restaurant, but most dining establishments in Trinoma stay open until 9-10 PM on weekdays and until 10-11 PM on weekends.

Do I need a reservation?

Most casual spots accept walk-ins, but popular restaurants like Gerry's Grill, Manam, and TGI Friday's may require a reservation at peak times.

Are there family-friendly options?

Yes, Trinoma is a family-oriented mall with many restaurants suitable for kids and large groups, including Filipino restaurants and casual dining options.
