## ðŸ“ Blog Post Formats

### Format A: Single Restaurant Deep Dive
*Best for: Featured restaurants, new openings*

Structure:
1. Hook + What makes it special
2. Location, hours, contact
3. Experience section (with images)
4. Menu highlights
5. The Experience (atmosphere, crowd, vibe)
6. Why It Stands Out (bullet points)
7. Visit Info + Social links
8. FAQs

**Examples:**
- Belcap's Big Tree Diner
- Wiltlover Cafe

### Format B: Neighborhood/Area Guide
*Best for: SEO traffic, comprehensive lists*

Structure:
1. Intro to the area
2. Top 10 restaurants in the area
3. Key info (hours, price range, cuisine type)
4. Insider tips
5. FAQs

**Examples:**
- Top 10 Tomas Morato Restaurants 2025
- Best Maginhawa Restaurants 2025
- Best Banawe Restaurants 2025
