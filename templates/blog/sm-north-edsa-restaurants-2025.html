Insider Tips for Dining in SM North EDSA

Make the most of your SM North EDSA dining experience with these local tips:

Parking Tips

Challenge: SM North EDSA can get crowded on weekends.

Solution: Arrive before 11AM or after 9PM for easier parking. The multi-level parking building has the most available spaces. Some restaurants offer parking validation with minimum spend‚Äîask before paying!

Best Times to Visit

Avoid the Rush:

  • Weekday Lunch (11AM-1PM): Popular with office workers but quieter than weekends
  • Early Dinner (5PM-6PM): Beat the dinner crowd and get better tables
  • Weekend Mornings: Perfect for brunch at PaperMoon Cafe or other cafes in The Block
  • Late Night (9PM+): Many restaurants stay open, and crowds thin out

Payment & Deals

What to Know:

  • Most sit-down restaurants accept credit cards and e-wallets
  • Food court stalls are mostly cash-only‚Äîbring small bills
  • ATMs available on every floor
  • Check restaurant social media pages for promos and discounts
  • Some restaurants offer birthday freebies‚Äîbring valid ID!

Reservations

When to Book Ahead:

  • Vikings: Book 1-2 days ahead for weekend dinners (walk-ins often have 1-2 hour waits)
  • Gerry's Grill: Reservations recommended for groups of 6+
  • Most Other Restaurants: Walk-ins usually fine, but call ahead for large groups

Food Court Strategy

Location: How to Get to SM North EDSA

SM City North EDSA is located along North Avenue, Quezon City, right next to the MRT North Avenue Station. It's one of the largest malls in the Philippines and easily accessible from various parts of Metro Manila.

Getting There

By Private Car

  • From EDSA: Take EDSA and exit at North Avenue, SM North EDSA is on the corner
  • MRT: Get off at MRT North Avenue Station - walking distance to SM North EDSA
  • Parking: Multi-level parking building with ample spaces
  • Parking Fee: ‚Ç±50 for first 3 hours

By Public Transport

  • MRT: MRT North Avenue Station - exit directly to SM North EDSA
  • Bus: Buses along EDSA stop at SM North EDSA
  • Grab/Taxi: Simply tell driver "SM City North EDSA"
  • Jeepney: Ride jeeps bound for "SM North EDSA" or "North Avenue" from various QC points
