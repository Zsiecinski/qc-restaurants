Eastwood Mall Restaurants: What to Expect

Eastwood Mall is the heart of the dining scene in the area. With three levels of restaurants, food courts, and specialty dining, you'll find everything from quick Filipino meals to international cuisines.

Ground Floor Dining

The ground floor hosts fast-food favorites and quick-service restaurants perfect for grab-and-go meals. You'll find familiar names like Jollibee, McDonald's, and local favorites.

Second Floor Fine Dining

Head to the second floor for sit-down restaurants including Vikings, Yakimix, and various specialty cuisines. This is where you'll spend more time but enjoy a fuller dining experience.

Eastwood Mall Food Court

The food court offers the most variety per square foot with stalls serving Filipino, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Western foods. It's budget-friendly and perfect when your group can't agree on one cuisine.

Citywalk 1 & 2 Dining Options

Beyond Eastwood Mall restaurants, **Citywalk 1 and Citywalk 2** offer open-air dining with a more relaxed vibe. These areas feature restaurants with al fresco seating, perfect for Quezon City's cool evenings.

Citywalk 1 Highlights

  • TGI Fridays - American bar and grill
  • Brother's Burger - Local burger chain
  • Torch Restaurant - Japanese teppanyaki

Citywalk 2 Favorites

  • Wee Nam Kee - Singapore Hainanese Chicken Rice
  • BONO - Korean restaurant
  • Sariwon Korean BBQ - All-you-can-eat Korean

Location: How to Get to Eastwood City

Eastwood City is located in Libis, Quezon City, easily accessible via C5 Road and Marcos Highway.

Getting There

  • By Car: From EDSA, take C5 northbound, exit at Eastwood. Free parking available.
  • By Jeep: Ride jeeps going to "Eastwood/Libis" from various QC points
  • By Bus: Buses along C5 stop near Eastwood
  • By Grab/Taxi: Simply tell the driver "Eastwood Mall" or "Eastwood Citywalk"
