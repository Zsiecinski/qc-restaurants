Insider Tips for Dining in SM Fairview

Make the most of your SM Fairview dining experience with these local tips:

Parking Tips

Challenge: SM Fairview can get crowded on weekends.

Solution: Arrive before 11AM or after 9PM for easier parking. The multi-level parking building has the most available spaces. Some restaurants offer parking validation with minimum spend‚Äîask before paying!

Best Times to Visit

Avoid the Rush:

  • Weekday Lunch (11AM-1PM): Popular with office workers but quieter than weekends
  • Early Dinner (5PM-6PM): Beat the dinner crowd and get better tables
  • Weekend Mornings: Perfect for brunch at Belle-Ville or Brewing Point
  • Late Night (9PM+): Many restaurants stay open, and crowds thin out

Payment & Deals

What to Know:

  • Most sit-down restaurants accept credit cards and e-wallets
  • Food court stalls are mostly cash-only‚Äîbring small bills
  • ATMs available on every floor
  • Check restaurant social media pages for promos and discounts
  • Some restaurants offer birthday freebies‚Äîbring valid ID!

Reservations

When to Book Ahead:

  • Vikings: Book 1-2 days ahead for weekend dinners (walk-ins often have 1-2 hour waits)
  • Gerry's Grill: Reservations recommended for groups of 6+
  • Most Other Restaurants: Walk-ins usually fine, but call ahead for large groups

Food Court Strategy

Maximize Your Experience:

  • Visit during off-peak hours (2-4PM) for shorter lines
  • Tables fill up fast during lunch‚Äîsend someone to grab seats first
  • Great for groups who can't agree on one cuisine
  • Most stalls offer combo meals for better value

Location: How to Get to SM Fairview

SM City Fairview is located in Greater Lagro, Quezon City, along Quirino Highway. It's one of the largest malls in the Philippines and easily accessible from various parts of Metro Manila.

Getting There

By Private Car

  • From EDSA: Take EDSA northbound, turn right to Quirino Highway, proceed to SM Fairview
  • From Commonwealth: Take Commonwealth Ave to Quirino Highway
  • Parking: Multi-level parking building with 2,000+ spaces
  • Parking Fee: ‚Ç±40 for first 3 hours

By Public Transport

  • Jeepney: Ride jeeps bound for "Fairview" from various QC points
  • Bus: Buses along Quirino Highway stop at SM Fairview
  • Grab/Taxi: Simply tell driver "SM City Fairview"
  • UV Express: Available from Cubao, Quezon Ave, and other QC areas
